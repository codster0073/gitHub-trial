Top 5 Car for Minnesota

These cars are great for anyone who is looking for a fast cool car that can put up with our minnesotan weather

Subaru-Wrx-Sti

The Subaru Wrx Sti is my overall number 1 choice. I choice this mainly because I believe it has the most value per dollar. First of all it is a couple steps away from becoming the perfect rally car. Performance wise it goes head to head with the evo lancer with 305hp. It is very mod freindly with companies such as cobb. Last of all subaru is known for its realiability. These cars have a qaulity build behind them and will last for hundreds of thousands of miles.

Mistibishu Evo Lancer

My overall second choice is the Evo Lancer. This car is incredibly quick and agile. It carries the rally look of the STI and the precision handling. It is a little more expensive used but with that comes its individuality. When put head to head with the wrx these cars are mere seconds apart

Mistibishu Eclipse

My third choice was the mistibishu eclipse. This car is much more budget friendly and a very unique look. With fwd is not the best for winter weather but works. and at half the price you have a car that is only a couple seconds behind on the quarter mile. It is very fun with a 3.0L turbo

Infiniti G35x

Coming in 4th the infiniti G35x. This car is much more comfortable or luxury like. Yet it still has its stunning looks. It is in a much different class compared to the rest of the cars. First of all it is much more rwd based. It will even send 100% of the power to the rear wheels during a steady speed. It is not as lightweigt with close to 500lb extra. This car is very fun if you need a more mainstream vehicle

Audi S4

My final choice is the audi S4. With amazing power and handling from german engineering this is one of the most technoligically advanced cars. This car makes 333hp from its supercharged 3.0L. This car will most certianly be the smoothest ride out of the 5. My only problem with this car is the fact that it is a gas guzzler. On top of that maintenance can get very spendy and sometimes repairs are triple the cost of much more common brands. If you can put that aside this is one of the nicest cars out of the 5 and will not let you performance-wise.
